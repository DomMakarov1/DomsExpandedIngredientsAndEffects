# Dom's Expanded Ingredients & Effects
 
A MelonLoader mod for Schedule I that adds new ingredients, a large chain of custom effects, and a secret vendor location to the game.
 
## Features
 
### New Ingredients
- **Moon Dust** — A mysterious lunar powder that defies gravity. Available at both Gas-Mart shops from Hustler V
- **Lipstick** — A glamorous cosmetic with transformative properties. Available at both Gas-Mart shops from Bagman III
- **Airhorn** — A chaotic party ingredient with unpredictable results. Available at both Gas-Mart shops from Enforcer I
- **Magic Juice** — A powerful mystical liquid with utility-focused effects. Available exclusively from The Alchemist from Enforcer IV
 
All Gas-Mart ingredients are also available for delivery via the phone delivery app.
 
---
 
### The Alchemist
A secret vendor stand hidden in the backyard of an unused building. Sells Magic Juice exclusively for $200 a bottle.
 
- Unlocks at **Enforcer IV**
- Appears on your map once you reach the required rank
- You'll receive a phone message from The Alchemist when you unlock it
- The stand features a glowing purple orb — you'll know it when you see it
 
---
 
### Moon Dust Effects
- **Helium Infusion** — Base effect applied by Moon Dust. Reduces gravity and increases jump height
- **Moon Gravity** — Mix two Moon Dusts together. Reduced gravity, increased jump, purple skin tint
- **Void** — Moon Gravity + Foggy. Slows movement, turns skin black
- **Alien** — Moon Gravity + Toxic. Camo green skin tint
- **Martian** — Two Moon Gravities combined. Beige-orange skin tint, high value boost
- **Super Martian** — Alien + Martian. Cycling green/orange skin, speed and jump boost
- **Bunny** — Helium Infusion + Energizing. 2x jump height, normal gravity
- **Nebula** — Helium Infusion + Sedating. Reduced gravity and speed, purple skin
- **Phantom** — Helium Infusion + Sneaky. Speed boost, dark navy skin
- **Wraith** — Phantom + Helium Infusion. Cycling navy/black skin, speed boost and reduced gravity
- **Tall** — Helium Infusion + Giraffying. 1.5x height
- **Inversion** — Tall + Foggy. Flips camera upside down for 30 seconds, flips NPCs visually
 
### Lipstick Effects
- **Voluptuous** — Base Lipstick effect. Enhanced body proportions, dramatic eyes, lip texture overlay, long eyelashes. Lasts 15 minutes
- **Gold Rush** — Voluptuous + Energizing. Full gold skin, gold lips, platinum hair. Lasts 15 minutes
- **Siren** — Voluptuous + Calming. Rose gold skin, speed boost, glamour glow. Lasts 15 minutes
- **Diva** — Voluptuous + Energizing (chain). Platinum blonde hair, glowing white eyes, speed boost. Lasts 15 minutes
- **Single Event Upset** — Voluptuous + Void. Displays a fake BSOD on screen for 10 seconds
- **Charged** — Voluptuous + Electrifying. Electric zap particles, yellow eyes, speed boost
- **Gilded Martian** — Gold Rush + Martian. Cycling gold/orange skin, speed and jump boost
 
### Airhorn Effects
- **Airhorn** — Base effect. Plays an airhorn sound on consumption
- **Troll** — Airhorn + Sneaky. Starts a hidden 5-minute timer then plays a knock sound
- **Fiesta** — Airhorn + Energizing. Party sound, confetti burst, speed boost
- **Creepy** — Troll + Airhorn. Plays suspenseful creepy music
 
### Magic Juice Effects
- **Magic** — Base Magic Juice effect. No value change — utility focused
- **Super Magic** — Mix two Magic Juices. Cycling purple/gold skin tint
- **Make It Rain** — Magic + Calming. Triggers a rain storm (NOT CURRENTLY FUNCTIONAL)
- **Super Charge** — Magic + Energizing. 2.5x speed, 3x jump for 10 minutes
- **Supercharge** — Super Charge + Super Magic. Same as Super Charge but lasts 30 minutes
- **Profit Boost** — Super Magic + Slippery. +15% profit on all drug sales for 10 minutes
 
More effects and ingredients are coming in future updates!

## Installation
1. Install MelonLoader for Schedule I
2. Download the latest release zip
3. Place the `.dll` file into your `Mods` folder (currently only available for Mono)
4. Place the `DomsCustomEffects` folder into your `Mods` folder (contains icons and sounds)
5. Launch the game
